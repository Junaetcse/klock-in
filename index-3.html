Klock In: A Simple Way For Your Employees To Clock In

Klock In is a quick, easy and free way to keep track of your employee working hours over a period of time, simply using your phone.

About Application

Klock In uses the phone’s front camera for check-in/check-out operations. The employer can to monitor the employee’s working hours, manage and edit them as required which makes it easier to track the working hours completed

KLOCK IN EMPLOYER:

Here you can add your company and enlist your employees using their phone numbers to track them. This app offers two modes:

Employee Mode: the employees get to clock-in/clock-out using the company phone. All the employees are listed under one platform, where the user selects his/her name and performs the operation. An employee can have multiple entries onfor the same day.

Employer Mode: the home page displays the information about the employees whereabouts i.e whether they are in office or working from elsewhere. It has features to generate weekly reports, separate logs for each employee and office hours are tangible.

Klock In Employee:

This app is hassle free for the employees, all the features that are not required for the employees to operate are discarded. Here the user can only perform the operations in house or remotely for their account only and monitor their logs. All the data are stored in the cloud and can be accessed from anywhere anytime.

Unique Features

  • Simple clock in/out using phone
  • Monitor, edit and add employees
  • Generate Reports

Use your personal devices for check in/out without running here and there or waiting in queues to do so.
